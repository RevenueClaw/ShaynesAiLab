## THE 8-TOOL STACK

### 1. ChatGPT (Free tier to start)
**What it does:** Your AI assistant for writing, brainstorming, analysis
**When to use:**
- Drafting emails that don't sound robotic
- Outlining blog posts and content
- Explaining complex topics simply
- Brainstorming when you're stuck

**Pro tip:** Use the voice feature to "talk" your ideas out while walking

**Cost:** Free (upgrade to Plus at $20/mo only if you hit limits)

---

### 2. Claude (Free tier)
**What it does:** Long-form documents and nuanced analysis
**When to use:**
- Analyzing contracts or long PDFs
- Writing detailed reports
- When you need more context than ChatGPT remembers

**Why it's different:** Larger "context window" - understands more of your document

**Cost:** Free

---

### 3. Grok (via X/Twitter)
**What it does:** Real-time info, current events, unfiltered answers
**When to use:**
- "What are people saying about [topic] right now?"
- Current news and trends
- When you want less corporate-speak

**Note:** This is my daily driver. But start with ChatGPT first.

**Cost:** Free tier available

---

### 4. Canva (Free)
**What it does:** Graphics, thumbnails, social posts
**When to use:**
- YouTube thumbnails (used to take me an hour, now 5 minutes)
- Instagram/Facebook graphics
- Quick presentations
- Logo mockups

**AI features:** Magic Write, Background Remover, Resize

**Cost:** Free (Pro at $13/mo if you need brand kit)

---

### 5. Fireflies.ai (Free tier)
**What it does:** Records and transcribes meetings
**When to use:**
- Never take meeting notes again
- Capture customer calls
- Remember what you agreed to

**My workflow:** Let it record, get the transcript, search for action items

**Cost:** Free tier (paid starts at $10/mo for more minutes)

---

### 6. Perplexity (Free)
**What it does:** AI search with sources
**When to use:**
- "What are the latest email marketing regulations?"
- Research with citations
- Fact-checking

**Difference from ChatGPT:** Perplexity shows WHERE it got the info

**Cost:** Free (Pro at $20/mo for more searches)

---

### 7. Notion (Free)
**What it does:** Notes, docs, databases, wikis
**When to use:**
- Project documentation
- Team collaboration
- Linking notes together

**AI features:** Ask questions about your notes, summarize

**Cost:** Free (AI features included)

---

### 8. Calendly (Free)
**What it does:** Scheduling without the back-and-forth
**When to use:**
- Booking calls with clients
- Team meetings
- Anytime you'd email "when works for you?"

**Saves:** 2-3 emails per meeting

**Cost:** Free (paid tiers for more event types)

## YOUR FIRST 30 DAYS

### Week 1: Pick ONE tool
Don't try to learn all 8. Pick the one that solves your biggest pain point:
- Drowning in email? → ChatGPT
- Always taking notes? → Fireflies
- Need graphics? → Canva

Use it for ONE specific task every day.

### Week 2: Add a second tool
Once the first feels natural, add one more.

### Week 3: Connect them
Start thinking about workflows:
- Fireflies records meeting → ChatGPT summarizes → Notion stores it

### Week 4: Review and iterate
What worked? What didn't? Cut what doesn't help.

How It Works

  1. Every few minutes, the workflow checks your Gmail inbox for new messages.
  2. Each message is classified: urgent, routine, promotional, or escalation.
  3. Urgent items alert you immediately via email or SMS.
  4. Routine items are batched into a nightly digest you review once.
  5. Promotional and low-priority email is filtered out of your attention.

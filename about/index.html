About

What This Site Is

Shayne's AI Lab is a resource hub for operations leaders at growing companies. We publish honest tool comparisons, practical automation guides, and free resources — all aimed at helping teams work more effectively without the hype.

Who Runs It

This site is run by someone who has spent years building and running operations systems for small-to-mid-size companies. The perspective here comes from real experience — what actually works when you're not a 500-person enterprise with a dedicated IT team.

We don't claim to have all the answers. What we do is share what we've learned, compare tools honestly, and flag both the strengths and the frustrations of the tools we cover.

Why It Exists

Most operations tool content falls into two camps: shallow listicles that don't compare anything meaningfully, or enterprise-focused consultant speak. We wanted a middle ground — a place where someone running operations for a 10-person company can get genuinely useful, specific information about what tools might work for them.

The site started as a consulting business. We're pivoting because the thing our visitors actually found useful was the tool comparisons and the starter kit — not the sales calls.

How We Make Money

This site uses affiliate links. If you sign up for a tool through one of our links, we may earn a commission — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site running and the content free.

  • We only recommend tools we believe are genuinely useful for our audience
  • We don't take sponsored placements or write paid reviews
  • We flag tools' real weaknesses, not just their strengths
  • When we compare two tools, our recommendation is based on what we think fits, not which pays more

The email nurture sequence and starter kit are free, no strings attached. We don't sell your data or run ads.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't do paid placements or sponsored content
  • We don't claim to have used every tool we list (we tell you when we have and when we haven't)
  • We don't publish AI-generated articles without real human editing and honest assessment
  • We don't do consulting or custom workflow builds anymore

Which Plan Should Ops Teams Pick?

Free Plan — Best for Testing

The free plan is surprisingly generous: up to 2,000 contacts, 3 funnels, 3 courses, and unlimited emails. This is enough to run a small lead generation funnel or a single course launch. The limitations (no custom domain, 3 automation rules) mean it's not a permanent solution, but it's excellent for evaluating the platform.

Startup ($27/mo) — Best for Most Teams

This is the sweet spot. Unlimited funnels, courses, products, and blog posts with 5,000 contacts and 50 automation rules. At $27/month (or $19/month billed annually), this replaces a website builder ($25), email platform ($13), funnel builder ($127), and course platform ($39) for a total saving of $177/month.

Webinar ($47/mo) — Best for Sales-Oriented Teams

The Webinar plan adds live and automated webinars plus A/B testing. If your ops workflow includes running live product demos, training sessions, or automated webinars, this is the tier to pick. The 10,000 contact limit and 100 automation rules handle most growing businesses.

Enterprise ($97/mo) — Best for Scale

At $97/month, you get 15,000 contacts, 500 automation rules, API access, and priority support. The API access is the key differentiator — it lets you connect Systeme.io to custom tools and workflows using Make.com or direct integrations.

Hidden Costs to Watch For

Systeme.io is refreshingly transparent about pricing, but there are a few things to be aware of:

  • Transaction fees: None. Systeme.io doesn't take a cut of your sales (unlike some competitors that charge 2-5% per transaction).
  • Migration costs: Moving from another platform isn't automated — you'll need to export/import contacts and rebuild funnels manually. Budget a few hours for this.
  • Email limits: While email sends are "unlimited," Systeme.io does have soft limits based on your contact count and sending reputation. Most users never hit these, but high-volume senders should check.
  • Custom domain: Included on all paid plans. SSL is also included and automatic.

Annual vs Monthly — The Math

Paying annually saves roughly 29% on every plan:

  • Startup: $27/mo → $19/mo ($228/yr, save $96)
  • Webinar: $47/mo → $33/mo ($396/yr, save $168)
  • Enterprise: $97/mo → $69/mo ($828/yr, save $348)

The 60-day money-back guarantee applies to both monthly and annual plans, so there's no risk in trying it for two months.

Bottom Line

Systeme.io is one of the few SaaS platforms where the value actually exceeds the price. The $27/month Startup plan delivers functionality that would cost $150-200/month spread across specialized tools. The free plan removes the risk of trying it. And the 60-day money back means you have two full months to decide if it works for your team.

For ops teams looking to consolidate their tech stack and save money, it's the easiest recommendation we can make this year.
